## Market Analysis Summary

### Price Points by Tier

**Entry/Basic Tier (Character-focused or limited):**
- Character.ai+: $9.99/mo
- Poe Basic: $4.99/mo (new tier)

**Standard Tier (Most common price point):**
- ChatGPT Plus: $20/mo
- Claude Pro: $20/mo
- Perplexity Pro: $20/mo
- Gemini Advanced: $19.99/mo
- Poe Subscriber: $19.99/mo
- Venice Pro: $18/mo

**Premium Tier:**
- ChatGPT Pro: $200/mo

### Key Observations

1. **$20/month is the industry standard** for full-featured AI chat subscriptions
2. **Venice.ai at $18/mo** is positioned as the affordable privacy-focused alternative
3. **Annual discounts** typically save 15-30% (effective $12-17/mo)
4. **Free tiers** are very limited (15-25 queries/day typical)
5. **Character.ai** is the cheapest at ~$10/mo but focused on character chat only

### Cost Analysis (for profitability)

With our Smart Router:
- ~60% queries → Free models (Groq Llama, DeepSeek R1) = $0
- ~30% queries → Grok 3 Fast = ~$0.0007/query
- ~10% queries → GPT-4o = ~$0.02/query

**Average cost per query: ~$0.003**

At Pro tier ($14.99/mo, 500 queries):
- Revenue: $14.99
- Cost: 500 × $0.003 = $1.50
- **Margin: 90%**

At Unlimited tier ($27.99/mo, ~1000 queries avg):
- Revenue: $27.99
- Cost: 1000 × $0.003 = $3.00
- **Margin: 89%**
