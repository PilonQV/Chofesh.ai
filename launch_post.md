## The Privacy Problem

At Chofesh.ai, we took a different approach:

- **Local-first storage**: Your conversations are encrypted and stored on your device
- **No data collection**: We don't train on your chats or sell your data
- **End-to-end encryption**: Your conversations stay between you and the AI

We believe AI should enhance creativity, not restrict it. Writers exploring dark themes, researchers studying sensitive topics, and creators pushing boundaries shouldn't hit arbitrary content walls.

Chofesh.ai provides access to uncensored AI models that respect your creative autonomy while maintaining ethical guidelines against genuinely harmful content.
