## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**1. Is my data truly private?**

Yes. Your data is stored locally on your device and is end-to-end encrypted. We cannot access your conversations, even if we wanted to.

**2. What happens if I clear my browser cache?**

Since your data is stored locally, clearing your browser cache will permanently delete your chat history. We recommend exporting your conversations if you need to keep a record of them.

**3. Can I access my conversations on multiple devices?**

Currently, your conversations are tied to the device and browser you use. We are exploring secure, privacy-preserving ways to enable multi-device sync in the future.

**4. How does Chofesh compare to other AI chat platforms?**

While other platforms may offer similar features, their business models often rely on collecting user data. Chofesh is different because our business model is aligned with your privacy. We offer a premium, privacy-focused service, not a free product where you are the product.

**5. What is uncensored chat?**

Uncensored chat means that we do not impose arbitrary content restrictions on your conversations, as long as they are within legal parameters. This allows for more natural and open discussions.
