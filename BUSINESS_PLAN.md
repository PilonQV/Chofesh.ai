## Key Risks & Mitigation

### Technical Risks

**1. Security Breach**
- Risk: Data leak, privacy violation
- Impact: Critical (brand damage)
- Mitigation: SOC 2, penetration testing, bug bounty
- Probability: Low

**2. Scalability Issues**
- Risk: Platform can't handle growth
- Impact: High (customer churn)
- Mitigation: Load testing, auto-scaling, CDN
- Probability: Medium

**3. Model Quality**
- Risk: AI models underperform
- Impact: Medium (user dissatisfaction)
- Mitigation: Multiple models, benchmarking, feedback
- Probability: Low

---

### Business Risks

**1. Competitive Pressure**
- Risk: OpenHANDS/Cursor gain market share
- Impact: High (slower growth)
- Mitigation: Faster innovation, privacy differentiator
- Probability: High

**2. Funding Gap**
- Risk: Can't raise Series A
- Impact: Critical (shutdown)
- Mitigation: Profitability focus, bridge financing
- Probability: Low

**3. Regulatory Changes**
- Risk: AI regulations limit features
- Impact: Medium (feature restrictions)
- Mitigation: Compliance team, legal counsel
- Probability: Medium

---

### Market Risks

**1. Market Saturation**

**2. Economic Downturn**
